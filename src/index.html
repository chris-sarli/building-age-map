How old is Providence?

By Chris Sarli

This is a map of parcels in Providence, stylized by the year of last building construction. All of the data comes from the City’s GIS Portal. This visualization was inspired by Kyril Negoda’s How old is Minneapolis?

Hovering on a parcel reveals the year, and clicking on it will open the relevant entry in Vision.
